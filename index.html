About me

Hi, I'm just your friendly neighborhood baker! I'm Janani, and I am currently a student. I used to try out recipes and get my classmates and friends to review them. Eventually, I found myself baking treats for class parties, leading to requests for small batches of cookies. I was curious as to how this would go as a mini-business and thus started J'bakes. If you are on this page, congratulations, because you just found your next sweet treat. Here's the catch: it's cheap too! I am only charging to break even as I am a relatively amateur baker, just trying this out;) I'm currently looking to go to classes and improve. Thank you for supporting your friendly neighbourhood baker!
